# Code of Ethics

This project is built on Christian virtues and aims to foster a community characterized by:

## Core Virtues

### Patience
We approach problems and people with patience, understanding that good solutions take time and that everyone is learning.

### Humility
We recognize our own limitations and are open to feedback. We don't assume we have all the answers.

### Service
We build software to serve others, not to glorify ourselves. The goal is to make people's lives better.

### Truthfulness
We are honest in our communications, documentation, and code. We don't hide problems or oversell capabilities.

### Charity
We assume the best intentions in others. When conflicts arise, we seek understanding before judgment.

## Practical Guidelines

1. **Be welcoming**: Everyone who wants to contribute constructively is welcome, regardless of background.

2. **Be respectful**: Disagree with ideas, not people. Avoid personal attacks or inflammatory language.

3. **Be helpful**: Share knowledge freely. Answer questions patiently. Help newcomers learn.

4. **Be honest**: Report bugs accurately. Don't claim credit for others' work. Acknowledge mistakes.

5. **Be thoughtful**: Consider the impact of changes on users. Think about edge cases and failure modes.

## Privacy Commitment

This project is designed with privacy in mind:
- All data stays within your Tailscale network
- AI processing happens via the bot (separate repo) — not in this server
- No telemetry, analytics, or tracking
- You control your own infrastructure

## Reporting Issues

If you experience or witness behavior that violates these principles, please reach out to the maintainers privately. We will address concerns with discretion and fairness.
